# 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Retail Sales Dataset to uncover meaningful business insights from transactional data.

Using Python and various data analysis libraries, the dataset was cleaned, explored, visualized, and analyzed to understand customer purchasing behavior, product performance, revenue trends, and sales patterns.

This project was completed as **Task 1** of the **Data Analytics Internship Program at Oasis Infobyte**.

---

# 🎯 Objective

The primary objectives of this project are:

* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
* Understand the structure of the dataset.
* Analyze customer demographics.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Analyze monthly revenue trends.
* Generate meaningful visualizations.
* Extract actionable business insights.
* Support data-driven business decision-making.

---

# ⚙️ Steps Performed

The following workflow was followed during the analysis:

### 1. Data Collection

* Imported the Retail Sales dataset into Google Colab.

### 2. Data Cleaning

* Checked missing values.
* Verified data types.
* Converted date columns into datetime format.
* Prepared the dataset for analysis.

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Performed statistical and graphical analysis to understand:

* Customer demographics
* Product categories
* Revenue distribution
* Monthly sales trends
* Age-based purchasing behavior
* Gender-wise spending patterns

### 4. Data Visualization

Created insightful visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn to communicate findings effectively.

### 5. Business Insight Generation

Interpreted analytical results and proposed business recommendations based on the observed patterns.

# 🔍 Key Insights

### Product Performance

* Clothing recorded the highest number of customer purchases.
* Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.

### Customer Insights

* Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.
* Customers aged **46–60 years** contributed the highest revenue.

### Sales Trends

* May 2023 recorded the highest monthly revenue.
* Revenue fluctuated throughout the year, indicating seasonal purchasing behavior.

### Product Preferences

* Female customers preferred Clothing and Beauty products.
* Male customers spent more on Electronics products.
